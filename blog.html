A Visit to the Federal Medical Center

posted on 16/03/2021

Her Excellency, Barr. (Mrs) Chioma Uzodimma took a personal tour of some of these facilities and is committed to ameliorating the situation. As part of the activities marking #NigeriaAt60 Independence Anniversary celebrations in Imo, the Imo State First Lady visited the three (3) wards at the Federal Medical Center (FMC) Owerri, namely: Newborn ward, Maternity Ward, Gynaecology Ward and undertook the medical bills of some indigent patients there. She donated food stuff, household items and financial support to the women, in each of these wards. She has also visited Mercy Homes Orphanage, Orji Obinze Love Care Orphanage Homes, Obinze and seized the opportunity to reach out to the orphans and motherless babies and endowed them with relief materials.
These outreaches have further exposed the Imo State First Lady to the situation of things at these facilities, which she did not find impressive. However, she has continued to liase with relevant authorities to ensure the restoration of these facilities and the provision of necessary tools/equipment for the optimum service delivery.

VISIT TO THE NIGERIA CORRECTIONAL SERVICE (NCS) CENTRES

posted on 16/03/2021

Our rehabilitation centres must continue to live up to their core mandate which is to reform minds and characters. This is also at the fore of the rehabilitation agenda of His Excellency, Governor Hope Uzodimma's administration. To this end, the wife of the Governor, Barr.(Mrs) Chioma Uzodimma has continued to identify with the reformation and rehabilitation process of Imolites at the Nigerian Correctional Service (NCS), Medium security Custodial Centres, Owerri and Okigwe by visiting these facilities. The visit was to inject a sense of renewed hope in their rehabilitation process by extending food items and financial support to the management on behalf of the inmates, including sundry gestures of goodwill to the inmates and members of staff.
In the words of the officials, the visit is the first of its kind in the history of the State. Apparently, Barr. (Mrs) Chioma Uzodimma is the very first First Lady to visit and felicitate with the officials and inmates of the NCS. It is believed that with the collaborative efforts of the State Government and the officials, these brothers and sisters of ours will be reformed and reintegrated into the society again, for good.
